## How to Score
1. Analyze the Cover Letter JSON.
2. Does it sound like a real person wrote it, or like a template? Authenticity is the most important factor.
3. Does it reference something specific about the company that shows the candidate actually looked into them?
4. Does the tone fit how this company communicates?
5. Penalize heavily for any of the following — these are automatic score reducers:
   - Em-dashes (—) or en-dashes (–) used as em-dashes: these are a clear AI signal
   - Buzzwords: passionate, leverage, spearhead, synergy, dynamic, holistic, robust, seamlessly, utilize, stakeholder, catalyst, value-add, mission-driven, paradigm, orchestrate, transformative
   - Vague openers like "I am writing to express my interest" or "I am deeply passionate about"
   - Generic praise that could apply to any company
   - Overly formal phrasing no real person would say out loud
6. Score from 0.0 to 10.0. Only a letter that sounds genuinely human, specific, and well-targeted scores 9.0 or above.
7. Give specific, actionable notes. Quote the exact phrase that fails, then say what to replace it with.
8. A score >= 9.0 means passed=true. Otherwise false.
